1. Create a Contest.
    - Title
    - Entrant instructions*
    - Email for final notification
    - Number of winners
    - Max number of entrants
    - Expiration time

    * Entrant Instructions:
        Provide LOTS of clear information here. Explain the contest, but more
        importantly, explain if users should sign up with an email address,
        or forum username, handle, or some other info, to be contacted
        as a winner.
        Markdown can also be used in this field.

2. Copy the sign-up link.

3. Send sign-up link to your friends.

4. When contest has expired, the winners are chosen randomly and emailed to you.
