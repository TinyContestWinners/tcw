Privacy Policy

Last modified: Nov 18th, 2022

At Tiny Contest Winners, privacy is taken very seriously. This policy outlines the measures taken by Tiny Contest Winners to protect the privacy of its users.

1. Service description

Tiny Contest Winners is a free web based service that allows users to create ephemeral contests, whose winners are picked at random.

2. How the contests are processed

The contest-signup link is generated in the contest creators browser. The link is thus in the creator's possesion only, and they determine who receives the contest link.

Once a contest has expired, the winners are picked at random. After notifying the contest owner by email who the winners are, all information about the contest is removed completely from the You Might Be A Winner database.

After a contest is deleted from You Might Be A Winner, there is absolutely no way to recover it again.

Tiny Contest Winners sysadmin team will do as much as possible to protect the site against unauthorized access, modification or destruction of the data.

3. Disclaimer

When a person clicks the Tiny Contest Winners link, Tiny Contest Winners declines any responsibility related to the contest's content.

4. Disclosure of Data to Third Party

Tiny Contest Winners does not share nor sell any information to others, nor use it in any way not mentioned in this Privacy Policy.

5. Use of cookies

Tiny Contest Winners uses cookies (small text files that are stored on your computer by your browser when you visit a website) for our own interest in improving the use of our site and service. In some cases they will also be used for promotional purposes. The type of cookies Tiny Contest Winners uses are listed below:

Functional cookies

Tiny Contest Winners uses persistent cookies to keep a session in the user's preferred language and to record your notification that Tiny Contest Winners uses cookies as explained in this section.

Non-functional cookies

Used for commercial and promotional purposes. Non-functional cookies are placed by third parties. In case of European citizens, these cookies do not store personal data (non-personalized ads).

If you would want to remove certain cookies, or block them from being stored in your browser, it is possible via your browser settings for cookies. However, if you do this, the site might not work as expected.

6. Validity of this Privacy Policy

Please note that this Privacy Policy may change from time to time. We expect most changes to be minor. Regardless, we will post any Policy changes on this page, and if the changes are significant, we will provide a more prominent notice such as a message on the home page. Each version of this Policy will be identified at the top of the page by its effective date.

7. Contact information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or other concerns regarding privacy issues, please send us an e-mail at and we will answer you in less than 5 working days. If you are not satisfied with the outcome of our communication, you may refer your complaint to a local supervisor authority.
